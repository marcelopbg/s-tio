# CORE STRUCTURE

## NYC is divided into 6 core experiences:

- Brooklyn waterfront (DUMBO / Williamsburg / Bushwick)
- Central Park (nature reset)
- Lower Manhattan (history + skyline density)
- West Village (human-scale NYC)
- High Line corridor (industrial-modern NYC)
- Roosevelt/Governors Island (escape NYC)

---

# VEGAN FOOD STRATEGY

## Best areas:
- Williamsburg (strongest)
- West Village (easy finds)
- Chelsea Market (variety)
- Flushing (Asian vegan options, but less curated)

---

# WHAT WE REMOVED ON PURPOSE

- Statue of Liberty ferry (time sink)
- Empire State Building (redundant views)
- Tourist shopping day
- Baseball game (not aligned with your interest)
- Overpacked multi-attraction days

---

# FINAL PHILOSOPHY

This itinerary is designed around:

- walking neighborhoods instead of ticking landmarks
- minimizing subway fatigue
- maximizing “city texture”
- leaving space for discovery
- avoiding tourist repetition loops

NYC is best experienced horizontally, not vertically.
